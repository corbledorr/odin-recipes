Egg Drop Soup

Egg drop soup, a simple dish that's honestly very basic but nonetheless, one that exists in the memory of every asian child. We're gonna pass down this tradition so follow closely.

    Ingredients

  • chicken stock
  • sesame oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • White pepper
  • tumeric
  • cornstarch
  • eggs
  • Scallion
  • MSG baby!

    Steps

  1. combine chicken stocks, sesame oil and everything except eggs together
  2. bring to boil
  3. Stir eggs in
  4. Turn off heat
  5. Stir aggressively until it looks like curtains
  6. Pour in bowl
  7. Take in aroma
  8. Reminisce about life as a kid
  9. いただきます!
